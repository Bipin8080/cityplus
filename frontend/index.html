How It Works

A four-step process involving three roles — Citizen, Admin, and Staff — to report, manage, and resolve civic issues.

01
person Citizen
edit_note

Submit an Issue

The citizen fills in the report form with a description, category, and location, and uploads a photo. The report is saved to the database with a status of Pending.

02
admin_panel_settings Admin
manage_search

Admin Reviews & Assigns

The administrator logs into the dashboard and reviews the submitted report. If accepted, the admin assigns the issue to an available staff member responsible for handling it.

03
engineering Staff

Staff Works on Issue

The assigned staff member receives the issue and carries out the required field work. They update the status to In Progress when work begins, and to Resolved once completed.

Once the staff marks the issue as Resolved, it is closed in the system. The citizen can view the final status through their dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the CityPlus platform.

How do I create an account and register on CityPlus?

expand_more

To create an account on CityPlus, click the "Login" button on the homepage and select the "Register" tab. Choose your role (Citizen or Staff), fill in your details including name, email, and password. Once your account is created, you can immediately start reporting issues if you're a citizen or access the dashboard if you're staff.

How do I track my report and get updates?

expand_more

Once you submit a report, it will be saved to your account. You can track your report by logging in to your Citizen Dashboard to view all your submitted reports with real-time status updates. Status updates are made by administrators and staff members as they review, assign, and work on issues.

What information should I include when reporting an issue?

expand_more

When reporting an issue, please provide: (1) A clear title or brief description of the problem, (2) Detailed description explaining the issue and its impact, (3) The exact location or address where the problem is located, (4) The appropriate category (potholes, garbage, water supply, streetlights, drainage, electricity, or vandalism), and (5) A clear photo or image showing the problem if possible. The more details you provide, the faster the issue can be addressed.

How long does it typically take for issues to be resolved?

expand_more

Resolution time varies depending on the severity and type of issue. After an issue is reported, the admin typically reviews it within 1-2 business days. Once approved and assigned to staff, resolution can take anywhere from a few hours to several weeks depending on the complexity and available resources. You can track the progress at any time using your dashboard. Critical issues affecting public safety are prioritized for faster resolution.

Is my personal information safe and private?

expand_more

Yes, your personal information is protected with the highest priority. Your name, email, phone number, and address are only accessible to the administrator and the staff member assigned to your issue. These details are not displayed on public issue listings. Your reported issues and descriptions are visible to authorized personnel only. We use secure encryption for data transmission and storage. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for complete details on how we handle your data.

Can I edit or delete my report after submission?

expand_more

Once a report is submitted, you cannot directly edit or delete it from the system. However, if you need to make changes or correct information, please contact our support team at cityplus.project@gmail.com with the details of your issue. They can assist you with updating critical information or addressing any issues with your report.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

expand_more

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in your email to set a new password. The link is valid for 1 hour. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support at cityplus.project@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

CityPlus collects personal information when you create an account and submit civic issue reports. This includes your name, email address, phone number, physical address, and any descriptions or photos you provide when reporting issues.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and track your reported civic issues
  • Communicate with you about your reports and resolutions
  • Verify your identity and prevent misuse of the platform
  • Improve our platform services and user experience
  • Generate anonymized statistics about civic issues

Information Visibility

Your personal contact information (name, email, phone number, address) is kept confidential and is only accessible to authorized administrators and the staff member assigned to resolve your issue. Your reported issue descriptions are visible to administrators and assigned staff. Public issue listings show only the issue category, location (general area), status, and submitted date – without any personal contact information.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption for data transmission and secure storage. Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and issue reports to maintain historical records and enable tracking. You can request data deletion by contacting our support team.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your information is shared only with administrators and assigned staff who need it to process your reports. This is an academic prototype platform not affiliated with any government or municipal authority.

Cookies and Analytics

CityPlus may use cookies to maintain your session and improve user experience. We do not use tracking cookies for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us at cityplus.project@gmail.com for any privacy-related requests.

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions, please email us at cityplus.project@gmail.com
