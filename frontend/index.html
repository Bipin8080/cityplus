Core Modules

CityPlus provides dedicated workspaces for citizens, field staff and administrators with secure, role-based access to civic service data.

Citizen Portal

Register, login and raise civic complaints with category, location and photo attachments. Track the progress and feedback on every issue in real time.

Staff Dashboard

View pending complaints, update status, add internal notes and mark issues as resolved after field verification.

Admin Analytics

Analyze complaint trends ward-wise and category-wise, identify hotspots, and generate summary reports for decision makers.

Notifications & Status

Citizens receive status updates (Open, In Progress, Resolved), improving transparency and trust with municipal authorities.
